Two pieces of information are needed in order to use this thalassaemia screening app:


1. Family history of a known thalassaemia case

2. Red blood cell parameters:
• Haemoglobin level (g/dL)
• Mean corpuscular haemoglobin value (pg)
• Mean corpuscular volume value (fL)
•Red blood cell count (x 10^12 /L)




This application is made as a guide to when thalassaemia diagnostic test (haemoglobin analysis) is indicated. We are not responsible for any errors or omissions, or for the results obtained from the use Of this application.
